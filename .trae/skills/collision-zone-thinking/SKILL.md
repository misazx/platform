# Collision Zone Thinking

## Overview

Innovation happens at the intersection of unrelated domains. Force collisions between your problem and distant fields.

**Core principle:** Combine two unrelated concepts and explore what emerges.

## The Process

1. **State the problem** clearly
2. **Pick a random domain** (nature, music, cooking, architecture)
3. **Find an analogy** - How does this domain solve similar problems?
4. **Force the collision** - Apply that solution to your problem
5. **Extract the insight** - What's the transferable principle?
